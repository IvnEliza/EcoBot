1. *Identify Tetra Pak Waste*🔍
   • Common examples include juice boxes, milk cartons, and soup cartons.

2. *Clean and Dry*💧
   • Rinse cartons to remove food or liquid residue, and let them dry.

3. *Flatten Cartons*🔄
   • Collapse cartons to save space in recycling bins.

4. *Remove Non-Carton Materials*↪
   • Remove caps, straws, and plastic spouts; recycle these separately if possible.

5. *Check Local Guidelines*🚮
   • Not all facilities accept Tetra Paks. Confirm with your local recycling program or look for designated drop-off points.
