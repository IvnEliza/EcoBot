1. *Keep It Clean*💧
   • Only recycle clean and dry cardboard.
   • Do NOT recycle cardboard with food stains (e.g., greasy pizza boxes) or excessive moisture.

2. *Flatten Boxes*🔄
   • Break down boxes to save space and ensure easier processing.

3. *Remove Non-Cardboard Materials*↪
   • Take off tape, labels, and plastic linings whenever possible.

4. *Separate Special Cardboard Types*❕
   • Heavily coated or waxed cardboard (like some frozen food boxes) usually cannot be recycled unless specified.

5. *Check Local Guidelines*🚮
   • Some facilities may have additional rules, such as limits on large quantities of cardboard.
