1. *Identify Cellophane*🔍
   • True cellophane is biodegradable and not plastic. Common examples include packaging for baked goods and some gift wraps.

2. *Check Local Guidelines*🚮
   • Cellophane is often not recyclable in standard facilities but may be compostable. Confirm with your local waste management.

3. *Clean and Dry*💧
   • Remove any food residue or moisture before disposal.

4. *Avoid Non-Recyclable or Mixed Materials*❗
   • Do NOT include plastic wraps labeled as cellophane substitutes or laminated cellophane.

5. *Composting Option*❕
   • If composting is available, dispose of true cellophane in your compost bin.
