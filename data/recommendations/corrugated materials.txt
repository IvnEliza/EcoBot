1. *Identify Corrugated Waste*🔍
   • Common examples are cardboard shipping boxes, packaging material, and corrugated trays.

2. *Clean and Dry*💧
   • Ensure the corrugated material is free from food, grease, or moisture.

3. *Flatten Boxes*🔄
   • Break down boxes to save space and make recycling easier.

4. *Remove Non-Cardboard Materials*↪
   • Take off tape, labels, and any plastic or metal fasteners.

5. *Avoid Non-Recyclable Corrugate*❗
   • Do NOT recycle wax-coated or heavily soiled corrugated cardboard unless accepted locally.
