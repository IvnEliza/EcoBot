1. *Identify Paperboard Items*🔍
   • Common examples are cereal boxes, shoe boxes, tissue boxes, and other lightweight cardboard.

2. *Clean and Dry*✨
   • Remove any food residue, grease, or moisture from paperboard items.

3. *Remove Non-Paper Materials*↪
   • Take off plastic liners, windows, or labels attached to the paperboard.

4. *Flatten Boxes*🔄
   • Break down paperboard boxes to save space.

5. *Avoid Non-Recyclable Paperboard*❗
   • Do NOT recycle waxed or heavily coated paperboard unless accepted locally.

6. *Check Local Guidelines*🚮
   • Some facilities may treat paperboard differently from other recyclables. Confirm local rules.
