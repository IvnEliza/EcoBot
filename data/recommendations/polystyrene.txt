1. *Identify Polystyrene (PS) Items*🔍
   • Look for the #6 recycling symbol. Common examples are foam packaging, disposable cups, takeout containers, and plastic cutlery.

2. *Clean and Dry*💧
   • Remove any food residue, grease, or liquids before recycling.

3. *Avoid Contaminated or Non-Recyclable Polystyrene*❗
   • Do NOT recycle:
     ◦ Dirty or greasy foam items.
     ◦ Colored, laminated, or mixed-material polystyrene.

4. *Check Local Guidelines*🚮
   • Many facilities do not accept polystyrene in curbside bins. Look for designated drop-off locations or special programs.
