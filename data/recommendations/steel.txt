1. *Identify Steel Items*🔍
   • Common examples include food cans, steel lids, and empty aerosol cans (if accepted locally).

2. *Clean and Dry*💧
   • Rinse steel items to remove food, grease, or liquid residue.

3. *Remove Non-Steel Parts*↪
   • Take off paper labels, plastic liners, or other attachments when possible.

4. *Avoid Non-Recyclable Steel Items*❗
   • Do NOT recycle:
     ◦ Rusty, contaminated, or oily steel.
     ◦ Mixed-material items (e.g., electronics or appliances).

5. *Check Local Guidelines for Large Items*🚮
   • Large steel items like tools or scrap metal may need to go to a dedicated recycling facility.
