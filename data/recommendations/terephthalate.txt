1. *Identify Terephthalate Items*🔍
   • Look for the #1 recycling symbol (PET or PETE). Common examples are water bottles, soda bottles, and some food packaging.

2. *Clean and Dry*💧
   • Rinse items to remove any food, drink, or residue.

3. *Remove Non-Plastic Parts*↪
   • Take off caps, labels, and rings; recycle these separately if accepted.

4. *Avoid Non-Recyclable PET Items*❗
   • Do NOT recycle:
     ◦ PET items contaminated with food or oil.
     ◦ Items mixed with other materials (e.g., multilayered packaging).

5. *Flatten Bottles (If Allowed)*🔄
   • Flatten bottles to save space, but check local guidelines to confirm this practice is accepted.
