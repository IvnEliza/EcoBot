1. *Identify Tin Items*🔍
   • Common examples are food cans, tin lids, and some metal containers.

2. *Clean and Dry*💧
   • Rinse tin items to remove food, grease, or liquid residue.

3. *Remove Non-Tin Parts*↪
   • Take off paper labels, plastic liners, or attached materials if possible.

4. *Avoid Non-Recyclable Tin Items*❗
   • Do NOT recycle:
     ◦ Rusty, oily, or heavily contaminated tins.
     ◦ Mixed-material items like electronics or coated tins.

5. *Check Local Guidelines for Large Items*🚮
   • Larger tin items may require special drop-off at a metal recycling facility.
