1. *Identify Recyclable Wood Waste*🔍
   • Acceptable: untreated, unpainted, uncoated wood (e.g., pallets, lumber, branches).

2. *Separate Treated or Contaminated Wood*❗
   • Do NOT recycle:
     ◦ Painted, stained, or pressure-treated wood.
     ◦ Wood with nails, screws, or other hardware (unless accepted locally).

3. *Clean the Wood*💧
   • Remove dirt, adhesives, or other residues before recycling.

4. *Check Local Guidelines for Disposal*🚮
   • Some facilities accept wood waste for composting or chipping, while others require drop-off at designated sites.
